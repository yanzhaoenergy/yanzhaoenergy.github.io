I am a Senior Engineer at Gas Technology Institute (GTI Energy), a nonprofit research institute in the Chicago area. I earned my Ph.D. in Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering from the University of California, Irvine. My research focuses on both renewable and fossil-fuel energy systems. I investigate both conventional fossil fuels (e.g., natural gas, propane, and coal) and low-carbon or renewable fuels (e.g., hydrogen, biogas, ammonia, and methanol), as well as electricity-integrated energy systems including microgrids, fuel cells, heat pumps, IC/gas turbine engines, and solar photovoltaic technologies.
